Automatic Gasoline Filling

MSME Project • September 2019

In Comparison with foreign countries, India considers that Labor is the biggest asset of Private and Government sector companies. Now a days however Automation of machines and software has replaced labour/employees. This is not because their working Manner is not good/superior compared to machinery but because of the cost and time efficiency of the latter option.

In some industries working methods in manufacturing products cause the employees some hazardous accidents and health issues. This is also one of the main reasons for Automation of processes and development of software. This increases work efficiency and also reduces accidents and health issues. The idea of bringing the automatic gasoline filling system in India is to reduce the man power and their associated risks, it is also a cost efficient method compared to the existing gasoline filling method.

This project enables the individual to use the gasoline pump all by himself from start to finish, It enables the person to know the exact current price per litre of fuel. Thereby chances of getting cheated or cheating is meaningfully reduced. The payment process is also an independent one wherein the money is paid beforehand in online modes or using the Cash counter as soon as the user enters the gasoline station. Hence all the procedures in the gasoline station end up being clean,Transparent and Authenticated. Simple and hassle free use is enabled.
