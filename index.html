Automatic Gasoline Filling

MSME Project • September 2019

Comparing to the foreign countries, India considered the Labors are the biggest asset of many Private and Government sector companies.Now a days the Automation of machines and software replacing the labors and employees it is not because of their working Manner not good compared to the machines but because of the cost and time efficiency of the machines. In some industries the working methods in manufacturing their products cause the employees and labors some hazardous health issues.This is also one of the main reason for the Automation of machines and software.Which does give you the work efficiency and also not problem about any health issues. The idea of bringing the automatic gasoline filling system in India is to reduce the man power and their risks,it is also a cost efficient method compared to the existing gasoline filling method.This project help the individual in using the gasoline pump all by himself,It enables them to know the exact litre:price ratio of the day.In this way the chances of getting cheated or cheating is very low. The payment process is also a independent one.Where the money is to paid earlier before in online modes or using the Cash counter when user enter the gasoline station. So in this way, all the procedures in the gasoline station is very clean,Transparent and Authenticated,It makes all of them who involve in this process feel free to use and process.
